Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

1. Overview

Comet keeps data collection intentionally small. We only store what we need to sign you in, manage access, and show scan results that were deliberately sent to this site.

2. Data we collect

Discord login: When you sign in, we receive your Discord user ID and basic profile info from Discord. We use this to attach pins/results to your account and to determine your role.

Pins & results: When a check runs, the Comet app sends scan data along with a pin to our API. We associate that result with the pin and with whoever generated it so it can be viewed on the site.

3. How we use data

We use your Discord ID to handle authentication and access control, and we use scan data only to render the results view in your dashboard. We don't sell your data or use it for ads. We also don't try to identify people outside what Discord already provides.

4. Cookies & sessions

Comet uses session cookies so you can stay signed in while you browse. We don't use third-party tracking pixels or ad cookies.

5. Data retention

Session information is kept only as long as necessary to keep you logged in. Scan results tied to a pin live in Comet's backend so they can be reviewed. We do not keep more history than we need for the product to function, and we may periodically clean up old data.

6. Your choices

You can sign out and stop using Comet at any time. If you have questions about what data we hold or want something removed, reach out through the Comet Discord server or support channels.

7. Changes

If we change how we handle data, we'll update this page and the date above. If you continue using Comet after that, it means you're okay with the updated policy.

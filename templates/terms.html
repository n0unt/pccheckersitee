2. What Zevora Does

Zevora is a forensic PC scanner designed to detect cheating in competitive Roblox flag football leagues. When run, it reads forensic artifacts from the local PC and transmits the results to the league agent who requested the scan. It does not install anything, does not run in the background, and is deleted after the scan completes.

Zevora may read and transmit the following types of information:

  • System and OS configuration and installation history
  • Records of programs run, installed, opened, or deleted (prefetch, registry, event logs, shellbags, recycle bin, and similar)
  • Roblox and Discord account identifiers and game log data
  • File system metadata including filenames, paths, and timestamps
  • Network adapter information and VPN indicators
  • FastFlag configuration data
  • Power on/off and logon/logoff session history

The creator of Zevora does not share scan results with any third parties outside of the requesting league. IP addresses are not stored or transmitted in publicly viewable reports.

How it works — Windows
1

Get a PIN

Your screenshare agent generates a one-time PIN from the Zevora portal and sends it to you in Discord.

2

Run ZevoraScanner.exe

Double-click the file. Windows will ask for admin permission — click Yes. This is required to read system forensic data.

3

Accept terms & enter PIN

Read and accept both consent boxes, enter your PIN, then click Start Scan.

4

Wait for completion

The scan runs automatically (2–5 minutes). Results are sent directly to your agent when done.

How it works — macOS
1

Get a PIN

Your screenshare agent generates a one-time PIN from the Zevora portal and sends it to you in Discord.

2

Open ZevoraScanner.dmg

Double-click the .dmg to mount it, then drag Zevora to your Applications folder. Right-click → Open on first launch if macOS shows a security warning.

3

Grant permissions when asked

macOS will ask for Full Disk Access or Accessibility permissions. These are required for the forensic scan — grant them in System Settings.

4

Accept terms & enter PIN

Read and accept both consent boxes, enter your PIN, then click Start Scan. Results are sent directly to your agent when done.

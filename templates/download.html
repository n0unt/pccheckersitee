How it works
1

Get a PIN

Your screenshare agent generates a one-time PIN from the Zevora portal and gives it to you in Discord or chat.

2

Run Zevora.exe

Double-click Zevora.exe. Windows will ask for admin permission — click Yes. This is required to read system forensic data.

3

Accept terms & enter PIN

Read and accept both consent boxes, then enter the PIN your agent gave you and click Start Scan.

4

Wait for completion

The scan runs automatically (2–5 minutes). When done, results are sent directly to your agent. You'll see a confirmation screen.

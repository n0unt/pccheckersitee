How it works
1

Get a PIN

Your screenshare agent will generate a one-time PIN from the Zevora portal and give it to you.

2

Run Zevora.exe

Double-click Zevora.exe. Windows will ask for admin permission — click Yes. This is required to scan system forensic data.

3

Accept terms & enter PIN

Read and accept both terms, then enter the PIN your agent gave you and click Start Scan.

4

Wait for completion

The scan runs automatically. When complete, results are sent directly to your agent. You will see a confirmation screen.
